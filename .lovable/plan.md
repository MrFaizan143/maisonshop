
## Asansol Momos — Website Plan

A warm, rustic e-commerce website for your momo stall, matching the wood/cream/gold aesthetic of your stall photo.

### Visual identity
- **Palette**: Warm cream (#FBF6EC) backgrounds, rich wood brown (#7A4E2D), gold accent (#C9A55C), deep charcoal text
- **Type**: Elegant serif for headings (Playfair / similar), clean sans for body
- **Logo treatment**: Circular badge "Asansol Momos" with the steaming momo icon, "Authentic Steamed Delights" tagline, "EST. 2023"
- **Textures**: Subtle wood-grain accents, warm string-light glow on hero, soft shadows

### Pages

**1. Home (`/`)**
- Hero: stall ambiance image, brand badge, tagline "The Authentic Dumpling Experience", CTAs → Order Now / View Menu
- Featured momo varieties (3–4 cards)
- "Freshly Steamed" value strip (Handmade • Steamed Daily • Authentic Recipe)
- Brand story teaser → links to About
- Catering/bulk-orders CTA banner
- Customer love (testimonial trio)

**2. Menu (`/menu`)** — *Shopify-powered storefront*
- Categorized: Steamed • Fried • Tandoori • Soup Momos • Combos
- Product cards with image, name, short description, price, "Add to Cart"
- Filter by category, vegetarian/non-veg toggle
- Cart drawer with quantity controls, subtotal, "Checkout" → Shopify Checkout

**3. About (`/about`)**
- Origin story of Asansol Momos
- The recipe & process (handmade, steamed daily)
- Photos of the team at the stall

**4. Gallery (`/gallery`)**
- Grid of momo varieties, stall shots, happy customers
- Lightbox on click

**5. Catering / Bulk Orders (`/catering`)**
- Packages (Office Lunch, House Party, Events)
- Inquiry form: name, phone, date, headcount, occasion, message
- Submissions stored in your database, viewable later

**6. Contact (`/contact`)**
- Stall location with embedded map
- Hours, phone, email, WhatsApp
- Social links
- Quick contact form

### E-commerce (Shopify)
- Full product catalog with variants (e.g., portion size: 6 pcs / 10 pcs)
- Inventory & stock management
- Cart + secure Shopify Checkout
- Pickup & delivery options
- Order management through your Shopify admin

### Global elements
- Sticky header: logo + nav (Home, Menu, About, Gallery, Catering, Contact) + cart icon with item count
- Footer: brand, quick links, contact, hours, socials, newsletter signup
- Mobile responsive with hamburger menu
- Smooth scroll, soft fade-in animations

### Placeholder menu (editable later)
Steamed Veg Momos, Steamed Chicken Momos, Fried Paneer Momos, Tandoori Chicken Momos, Schezwan Momos, Cheese Corn Momos, Momo Soup Bowl, Family Combo Pack — all with sample prices and descriptions you can edit anytime.

### Setup needed
- **Shopify** integration to power the menu, cart, and checkout (you'll be prompted to create a new store or connect an existing one — a free dev store works while building)
- **Lovable Cloud** for the catering inquiry form submissions
